Bhuvneshwar Kumar, fondly known as 'Bhuvi,' is a premier Indian fast bowler renowned for his exceptional ability to swing the ball both ways, especially with the new ball. Debuting in 2012, he became a key limited-overs specialist, winning consecutive IPL Purple Caps (2016, 2017).
